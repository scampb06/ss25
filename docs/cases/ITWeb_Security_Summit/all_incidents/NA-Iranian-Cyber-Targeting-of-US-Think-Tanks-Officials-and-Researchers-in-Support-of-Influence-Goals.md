## Incident Summary

**Title:** Iranian cyber targeting of U.S. think tanks, officials, and researchers in support of influence goals  
**Date:** 2022  
**Targets:** U.S. think tanks, Western officials, journalists, researchers, and public-policy communities  
**Continent:** North America  
**Perpetrator:** APT42 linked to Iran, as captured in the uploaded cyber-operations dataset  
**Motivation:** Espionage, strategic influence, and access to information relevant to public debate and policy narratives  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Credential phishing and account-targeting operation  
- **Systems Attacked:** Email accounts and digital communications of think tanks, officials, journalists, and researchers  
- **Data Held:** Policy communications, contact lists, research, and personal account content  
- **Availability Impact:** No major outage publicly reported  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Targeted access and collection activity were reported; exact exfiltration volumes not enough info  
- **Estimated Impact:** Exposure risk for policy discussions and strategic communications  
- **Investigators:** Security researchers and dataset compilers  
- **Remediation:** Public warnings, stronger authentication, threat hunting, and awareness measures  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** The targeting aligned with a broader hybrid influence environment in which access to journalists and think tanks can be used to shape narratives and gather material for information operations  
- **Narratives:** Not enough info on a single associated narrative package in the cited dataset entry  
- **Deception:** Social engineering and impersonation for account compromise  
- **Platforms:** Email and online account infrastructure  
- **Reach:** High-value U.S. public-policy and media communities  
- **Impact:** Elevated risk to information integrity and policy discourse  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** Security analysts and public reporting  
- **Remediation:** Public disclosure and defensive guidance  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The cyber targeting focused on actors central to public discourse and influence ecosystems

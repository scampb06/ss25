## Incident Summary

**Title:** Chinese interference networks and digital manipulation affecting Canadian elections  
**Date:** 2019-2024  
**Targets:** Canadian democratic institutions, federal elections, diaspora communities, public discourse  
**Continent:** North America  
**Perpetrator:** PRC-linked influence networks and associated digital interference activity as examined by Canadian inquiries and reporting  
**Motivation:** Shape political outcomes, influence elite and community opinion, and steer discourse favorable to Beijing  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Digital interference, online targeting, and cyber-enabled information operations; not enough info on a single confirmed destructive breach in the cited material  
- **Systems Attacked:** Election-related information environment, online communication ecosystems, community information channels  
- **Data Held:** Not enough info  
- **Availability Impact:** Not enough info on outages  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Not enough info on exfiltration in the cited sources  
- **Estimated Impact:** Subtle but potentially effective distortion of democratic discourse  
- **Investigators:** Canadian intelligence agencies, public inquiry structures, journalists  
- **Remediation:** Inquiry findings, warnings, and protective measures around democratic resilience  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** Systematic disinformation and influence targeting through open-source and networked digital channels  
- **Narratives:** Pro-Beijing themes, candidate-specific framing, and narrative shaping within targeted communities  
- **Deception:** Covert networked amplification and digital manipulation  
- **Platforms:** Open-source online platforms and community information channels  
- **Reach:** Targeted but politically meaningful reach  
- **Impact:** Distorted perception of candidates and democratic processes  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** Canadian inquiry bodies, journalists, intelligence agencies  
- **Remediation:** Exposure, public reporting, and institutional countermeasures  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The manipulation operated through cyber-enabled digital ecosystems central to the interference effort

## Incident Summary

**Title:** Russian-linked digital influence and suppression planning around Sudanese protests  
**Date:** 2018-2019  
**Targets:** Sudanese protesters, opposition discourse, and Sudan's political information environment  
**Continent:** Africa  
**Perpetrator:** Russian-linked actors associated with Prigozhin-linked operations, according to CNN and dataset references  
**Motivation:** Preserve friendly political influence, suppress anti-regime protest momentum, and expand Russian leverage  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Cyber-enabled information operation planning, including digital monitoring, online messaging coordination, and manipulation of social-media discourse  
- **Systems Attacked:** Social-media ecosystems and protest-related online discourse; not enough info on a confirmed destructive infrastructure breach  
- **Data Held:** Protest narratives, audience engagement patterns, and online mobilization content  
- **Availability Impact:** No major outage required  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Not enough info on exfiltration; the operation centered on covert digital messaging and suppression support  
- **Estimated Impact:** Helped shape online narratives against protest movements and in favor of regime stability  
- **Investigators:** CNN, Stanford/Graphika-linked research streams, and Africa-focused disinformation datasets  
- **Remediation:** Public reporting and ongoing scrutiny of foreign interference  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** Coordinated propaganda and narrative manipulation in support of Sudanese authorities and Russian interests  
- **Narratives:** Anti-protest themes, regime-stability messaging, and delegitimization of demonstrators  
- **Deception:** Covert messaging coordination, manipulated narratives, and foreign-backed strategic communications support  
- **Platforms:** Social media and digital messaging channels  
- **Reach:** National  
- **Impact:** Added pressure against protest information flows and public mobilization  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** CNN, researchers, and Africa-focused monitoring groups  
- **Remediation:** Exposure through investigative reporting  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The operation used cyber-enabled social-media infrastructure to execute strategic disinformation and suppression support

## Incident Summary

**Title:** Regional disinformation and propaganda flows from extra-regional state media into South American information environments  
**Date:** 2021 and after  
**Targets:** Audiences in Argentina, Colombia, Uruguay, Venezuela, and the broader South American Spanish-language space  
**Continent:** South America  
**Perpetrator:** Russia-, China-, and allied state-media ecosystems identified in regional disinformation research  
**Motivation:** Expand geopolitical influence and shape regional narratives  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Cyber-enabled dissemination networks, coordinated inauthentic behavior, and industrial-scale digital propaganda distribution  
- **Systems Attacked:** Social-media platforms, digital news ecosystems, and messaging channels  
- **Data Held:** Content networks, amplification accounts, and message-distribution assets  
- **Availability Impact:** No outage required  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Not enough info on one incident-specific exfiltration pattern  
- **Estimated Impact:** Sustained manipulation of South American political and geopolitical discourse  
- **Investigators:** Global Americans and regional disinformation researchers  
- **Remediation:** Exposure, fact-checking, and policy recommendations  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** Propaganda and disinformation distributed through digital media and social channels  
- **Narratives:** Pro-Russia, pro-China, anti-U.S., and region-specific political narratives  
- **Deception:** Coordinated inauthentic behavior, cyborg accounts, and manipulated narrative amplification  
- **Platforms:** Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, WhatsApp, and Telegram  
- **Reach:** Region-wide  
- **Impact:** Degraded trust in information integrity and influenced perceptions on international issues  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** Global Americans and cited regional analysts  
- **Remediation:** Monitoring and resilience-building proposals  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The influence efforts rely on cyber-enabled account networks, platform manipulation, and industrial distribution architecture

## Incident Summary

**Title:** Russian active measures against the United States and allied North American interests  
**Date:** 2016-2025  
**Targets:** U.S. public discourse, democratic institutions, allied North American information environments  
**Continent:** North America  
**Perpetrator:** Russian military and intelligence agencies  
**Motivation:** Destabilize Western democratic cohesion, shape strategic perceptions, and weaken trust in institutions  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Offensive cyberattacks, espionage, digital probing, and hybrid cyber operations  
- **Systems Attacked:** U.S. political and strategic systems, allied digital infrastructure; exact systems vary across incidents  
- **Data Held:** Political data, institutional data, strategic communications  
- **Availability Impact:** Varies by incident; not enough info for a single consolidated outage picture  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Documented in multiple incidents, including political hacks and espionage operations  
- **Estimated Impact:** Sustained strategic pressure on North American democratic resilience  
- **Investigators:** U.S. agencies, allied security bodies, think tanks, journalists  
- **Remediation:** Attribution, sanctions, resilience-building, election protections  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** Long-running active measures and disinformation paired with cyber operations  
- **Narratives:** Polarizing domestic political narratives, anti-Western themes, false geopolitical claims  
- **Deception:** Personas, cutouts, fake outlets, covert amplification  
- **Platforms:** Social media, websites, leak infrastructures, media ecosystems  
- **Reach:** Wide U.S. and broader North American reach  
- **Impact:** Deepened mistrust and social polarization  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** CSIS analysts, U.S. and allied researchers  
- **Remediation:** Exposure, public attribution, platform moderation, strategic communications  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** Russian active measures repeatedly combine offensive cyber activity with disinformation and other influence tactics

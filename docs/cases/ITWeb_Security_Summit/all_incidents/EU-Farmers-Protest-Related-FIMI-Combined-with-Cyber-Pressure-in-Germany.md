## Incident Summary

**Title:** Farmers' protest related FIMI combined with cyber pressure in Germany  
**Date:** 2024  
**Targets:** German coalition government, protest information space, public debate in Germany  
**Continent:** Europe
**Perpetrator:** Russia-linked FIMI ecosystems and associated cyber-enabled amplification networks  
**Motivation:** Exploit social unrest, polarize debate, weaken trust in the German government  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Cyber-enabled amplification and hostile digital infrastructure use; not enough info on a single destructive intrusion  
- **Systems Attacked:** Online information channels and impersonation-style infrastructure  
- **Data Held:** Not enough info  
- **Availability Impact:** Not enough info on outages  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Not enough info  
- **Estimated Impact:** Elevated hybrid pressure around a politically sensitive event  
- **Investigators:** EEAS and open-source researchers  
- **Remediation:** Exposure, monitoring, and public awareness measures  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** Coordinated FIMI targeting Germany around the farmers' protests  
- **Narratives:** Anti-government, anti-EU, and divisive messaging tied to social grievances  
- **Deception:** Coordinated inauthentic amplification and manipulative framing  
- **Platforms:** X, websites, other social platforms  
- **Reach:** National discourse  
- **Impact:** Polarization and distortion of protest-related public debate  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** EEAS analysts  
- **Remediation:** Exposure and counter-FIMI monitoring  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The operation used cyber-enabled dissemination infrastructure as part of the influence effort

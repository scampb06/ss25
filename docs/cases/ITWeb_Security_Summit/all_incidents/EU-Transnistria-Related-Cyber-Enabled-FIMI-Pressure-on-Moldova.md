## Incident Summary

**Title:** Transnistria-related cyber-enabled FIMI pressure on Moldova  
**Date:** 2024  
**Targets:** Moldovan government, President Maia Sandu, Moldovan public discourse  
**Continent:** Europe
**Perpetrator:** Russia-linked FIMI ecosystems according to EEAS reporting  
**Motivation:** Destabilize Moldova, intensify fears tied to Transnistria, and derail EU integration  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Cyber-enabled dissemination and hostile online infrastructure use  
- **Systems Attacked:** Online information channels and influence infrastructure  
- **Data Held:** Not enough info  
- **Availability Impact:** Not enough info  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Not enough info  
- **Estimated Impact:** Increased political pressure and manipulation of security perceptions  
- **Investigators:** EEAS and Moldovan authorities  
- **Remediation:** Public exposure, resilience measures, and strategic communication  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** Repeated attacks linked to tensions in Transnistria and false claims about Moldova and the war in Ukraine  
- **Narratives:** Moldova being dragged into war, anti-Sandu smears, anti-EU themes  
- **Deception:** Manipulative stories tied to geopolitical fear and crisis exploitation  
- **Platforms:** Online channels, social media, websites  
- **Reach:** National and regional  
- **Impact:** Heightened polarization and insecurity  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** EEAS analysts and Moldovan partners  
- **Remediation:** Exposure and counter-FIMI messaging  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The influence effort relied on cyber-enabled online infrastructure and coordinated dissemination

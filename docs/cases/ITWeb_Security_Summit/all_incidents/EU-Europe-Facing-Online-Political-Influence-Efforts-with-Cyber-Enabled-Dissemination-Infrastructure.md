## Incident Summary

**Title:** Europe-facing online political influence efforts with cyber-enabled dissemination infrastructure  
**Date:** Multiple years through 2023  
**Targets:** European political actors, voters, and issue publics in multiple countries  
**Continent:** Europe
**Perpetrator:** Multiple foreign influence actors catalogued in the uploaded online political influence efforts dataset documentation  
**Motivation:** Shape political attitudes, influence elections and policy debates, and weaken trust in democratic institutions  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Cyber-enabled dissemination, fake-account operations, covert online coordination, and other digital support functions; not all cases involve destructive intrusion  
- **Systems Attacked:** Social-media ecosystems, online political discourse, and platform integrity  
- **Data Held:** Account networks, audience data, and targeted political messaging artifacts  
- **Availability Impact:** No major outages required  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Not enough info for a single incident-level exfiltration pattern because this is a cross-case analytical source  
- **Estimated Impact:** Persistent manipulation of European political discourse  
- **Investigators:** The dataset authors and associated researchers  
- **Remediation:** Platform takedowns, public exposure, and democratic-resilience measures  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** Online political influence efforts involving covert coordination and manipulated content distribution  
- **Narratives:** Election-related narratives, geopolitical narratives, and issue-specific manipulation varying by case  
- **Deception:** Fake personas, covert sponsorship, coordinated amplification, and authenticity laundering  
- **Platforms:** Social media and web platforms  
- **Reach:** Varies by campaign and country  
- **Impact:** Distorted public discourse and trust erosion  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** Academic researchers and platform investigators  
- **Remediation:** Exposure, platform disruption, and public education  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The operations relied on digital infrastructures and covert online coordination that bridge cyber-enabled tactics and FIMI methods

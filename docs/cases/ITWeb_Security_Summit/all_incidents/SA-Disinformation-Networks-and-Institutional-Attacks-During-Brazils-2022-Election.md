## Incident Summary

**Title:** Disinformation networks and institutional attacks during Brazil's 2022 election  
**Date:** 2022  
**Targets:** Brazilian voters, Lula and Bolsonaro campaigns, and confidence in the Superior Electoral Court  
**Continent:** South America  
**Perpetrator:** Domestic online disinformation networks; some broader regional patterns were also documented by Reuters and rights groups  
**Motivation:** Shape electoral perceptions, undermine trust in the electoral authority, and polarize society  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Coordinated digital dissemination, platform manipulation, and video-first social propagation  
- **Systems Attacked:** TikTok, Instagram, WhatsApp, and other election-information channels  
- **Data Held:** Political content and audience-targeting/distribution networks  
- **Availability Impact:** No outage required  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Not enough info on exfiltration  
- **Estimated Impact:** Undermined trust in the neutrality of the national electoral body and intensified campaign toxicity  
- **Investigators:** Reuters, Carter Center, Brazilian rights groups, and election observers  
- **Remediation:** Court takedown orders, platform action, and election-integrity monitoring  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** Sophisticated online disinformation attacking candidates and the electoral process  
- **Narratives:** Electoral fraud allegations, candidate smears, and distrust in election management  
- **Deception:** Misleading video content, coordinated social amplification, and false claims about the voting system  
- **Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram, WhatsApp, and other major platforms  
- **Reach:** National  
- **Impact:** Further eroded confidence in election administration  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** Reuters, Carter Center, and Brazilian civil-society monitors  
- **Remediation:** Accelerated removal policies and court intervention  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The campaign depended on cyber-enabled distribution infrastructures and coordinated online amplification

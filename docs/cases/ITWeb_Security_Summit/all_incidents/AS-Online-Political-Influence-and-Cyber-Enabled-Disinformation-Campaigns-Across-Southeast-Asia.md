## Incident Summary

**Title:** Online political influence and cyber-enabled disinformation campaigns across Southeast Asia  
**Date:** 2017-2023  
**Targets:** Voters, issue publics, and political institutions in Southeast Asian states  
**Continent:** Asia  
**Perpetrator:** Multiple foreign and domestic influence actors catalogued in regional and academic reporting  
**Motivation:** Shape public attitudes, influence political outcomes, and control information environments  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Cyber-enabled dissemination infrastructure, fake accounts, coordinated online manipulation, and platform exploitation  
- **Systems Attacked:** Social-media ecosystems, digital public spheres, and platform integrity  
- **Data Held:** Account networks, political messaging content, and audience-targeting data  
- **Availability Impact:** No major outages required  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Not enough info on one consolidated exfiltration pattern; the emphasis is on manipulation infrastructure  
- **Estimated Impact:** Persistent distortion of political discourse across multiple Southeast Asian countries  
- **Investigators:** Academic researchers, platform investigators, and regional policy analysts  
- **Remediation:** Fact-checking efforts, platform actions, digital-literacy measures, and policy proposals  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** Coordinated fake news and disinformation campaigns in online environments  
- **Narratives:** Election narratives, polarizing political claims, crisis misinformation, and state-favorable messaging  
- **Deception:** Fake accounts, manipulated content, covert sponsorship, and organized amplification  
- **Platforms:** Social media and online media ecosystems  
- **Reach:** Multi-country regional reach  
- **Impact:** Democratic distrust and degraded information integrity  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** ASEAN-related analysts, academic researchers, policy institutions  
- **Remediation:** Fact-checking networks, policy interventions, platform moderation, public education  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The manipulation campaigns relied on cyber-enabled online infrastructures and covert digital coordination

## Incident Summary

**Title:** U.S. election disinformation and cyber-enabled pre-positioning around biolabs narratives  
**Date:** 2021-2022  
**Targets:** U.S. public opinion, trust in U.S. institutions, views of U.S. foreign policy  
**Continent:** North America  
**Perpetrator:** Russian disinformation operators with cyber-enabled dissemination infrastructure  
**Motivation:** Sow division, undermine U.S. credibility, and pre-position false narratives for strategic effect  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Cyber-enabled dissemination and manipulation infrastructure rather than a single destructive intrusion  
- **Systems Attacked:** Online information ecosystem and digital distribution channels  
- **Data Held:** Not enough info on specific compromised datasets  
- **Availability Impact:** No major outage reported  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Not enough info on exfiltration; main technical component was networked online propagation  
- **Estimated Impact:** Increased public confusion and narrative manipulation around national-security issues  
- **Investigators:** Microsoft researchers, CFR analysts, public-interest researchers  
- **Remediation:** Prebunking, exposure, fact-checking, and counter-disinformation analysis  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** False narrative seeding using digital channels and coordinated amplification  
- **Narratives:** U.S. bioweapons activity and malign secret programs  
- **Deception:** False claims framed around partial truths and pushed through online influence networks  
- **Platforms:** YouTube and broader online ecosystems  
- **Reach:** Wide English-language and international audiences, including North American audiences  
- **Impact:** Distorted understanding of U.S. activities and increased mistrust  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** CFR analysts, Microsoft, researchers  
- **Remediation:** Early exposure and explanatory counter-messaging  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The influence campaign relied on cyber-enabled dissemination networks and digital manipulation infrastructure

## Incident Summary

**Title:** Suspected Russian-linked attempt to interfere in South Africa's 2019 election discourse  
**Date:** 2019  
**Targets:** South African election discourse, party politics, and online public opinion  
**Continent:** Africa  
**Perpetrator:** Suspected Russian-linked influence actors discussed in South African reporting and broader Africa disinformation tracking  
**Motivation:** Shape political perceptions and expand Russian influence in South Africa  

## Cyberattack Details

- **Nature:** Cyber-enabled influence operation rather than a publicly confirmed destructive intrusion  
- **Systems Attacked:** Social-media discourse and digital political communication channels  
- **Data Held:** Not enough info on compromised datasets; available reporting centers on influence architecture  
- **Availability Impact:** No major outage reported  
- **Data Exfiltration/Alteration:** Not enough info on exfiltration  
- **Estimated Impact:** Heightened concern over foreign manipulation of South African electoral discourse  
- **Investigators:** Daily Maverick, South African analysts, and Africa-focused disinformation monitors  
- **Remediation:** Public reporting and vigilance around election integrity  

## Disinformation Details

- **Nature:** Suspected coordinated foreign influence in the election information environment  
- **Narratives:** Russia-friendly geopolitical messaging and influence-seeking narratives around South African politics  
- **Deception:** Covert online amplification and possible inauthentic political messaging  
- **Platforms:** Social media and online political channels  
- **Reach:** National  
- **Impact:** Raised foreign-interference concerns in a democratic setting  
- **Disinfo Investigators:** Daily Maverick and local monitoring groups  
- **Remediation:** Public exposure and election-integrity monitoring  
- **Link to Cyberattack:** The case is cyber-enabled through covert digital influence infrastructure, though public evidence for intrusive hacking remains limited
